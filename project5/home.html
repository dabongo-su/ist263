Defining Industrial Design

Industrial design, the profession of designing products ranging from ornamental objects to usable products. Its important for designers to see the world from an outside perspective. This gives insight to see how things work, and to observe the different habits and perspectives people have. Through these observations designers can make huge innovative changes for a growing society. Observation and research can lead to purposeful, creative, and long lasting products.
