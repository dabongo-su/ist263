Compactable Bike Helmet

Idea

My goal for this project was to create a helmet that is protective and forms to my head. I decided to design my helmet for the everyday cyclist. People ride bikes as a means of everyday transportation. Given that biking is so common and frequent I decided to design and prototype a more compact helmet. My folding helmet will make its transportation easier. Itâ€™s very common for people to not wear a helmet because of the burden of its bulkiness. According to safety.fhwa.dot.gov only 18% of bicyclists wear bike helmets. In this case convenience and protection are at cost. I believe if bike helmets were easier to store, people would wear them much more often. My hope is to increase the percentage of people wearing bike helmets while also still protecting them from any accidents.

Materials

  • (Ethylene-vinyl acetate) EVA Foam
  • Aqua-Resin
  • Fiber Glass Paper
  • 2 Washers
  • 2 Nails

Process

First front and side profile pictures were taken and transformed in phtotoshop to size the images acurately. Once the images were printed, I made a foamcore stand that was used guide the shape of the helmet to fit my head. I then taped my head to get the exact form. Once the tape formation was cut into the shapes I needed I used its measurements to translate it into the foam pieces. Once the foam was cut into the correct shapes, I used a heat gun to bend each piece. With each piece in the correct shape, I lined the pieces with fiber glass paper and aqua resin in multiple layers. When the pieces were hardened, I sanded them until it was completely smooth. Then i used two nails and washers to combine the pieces where they could move. Finally the helmet was spray painted and the straps were attached.
